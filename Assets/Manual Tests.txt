# The Tests That Should Pass
Format: given, when, then.

## When In Keyboard Mode Only
1. When the player moves, then mouse the cursor moves smoothly.
1. When the cursor passes over an enemy, the enemy highlights.
1. When the cursor passes over an enemy, the cursor disappears.
1. When the ground is clicked, the player moves to the position.
1. When the mouse is over any slope, the cursor matches the slope.
1. When the mouse is over a "very steep" slope, the cursor hides(1)

## Notes
1. Consider: In keyboard mode, When the mouse is over a non-navigable slope, the cursor hides.
